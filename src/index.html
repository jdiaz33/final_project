Origins of Microsoft is a website based on the film "Triumph of THE NERDS" which was released in June, 1996. Starring Robert X. Cringely who is a technology journalist and have written magazine articles about the pioneers of the computer industry. In the film he tells the story of how personal computers were born and he interviews a bunch of guys that he call nerds but that in today's world they have made history, they are multimillionaires and own this huge industry.

At the time when personal computers were created, several of these founders of the PC revolution started their own companies such as Apple Computer and Microsoft. The film explains the general concept of the creation of personal computers speaking of the different founders of these companies, but this website will focus only on the achivements of Microsoft and their founders. How they started and what was their individual role in the invention.

Normally when people hear the name Microsoft, they immediately relate it to only one person, the famous Bill Gates. But according to the film, there are another two gentelmen that also played a huge role on the invention of the first computer and the creation of Microsoft. We are talking about Paul Allen and Steve Ballmer who along with Bill gates founded what todays is worth over $226 billion.
