Steven Anthony "Steve" Ballmer born on March 24, 1956, is an American businessman. At college, Ballmer was a manager for the football team, worked on The Harvard Crimson newpaper as well as the Harvard Advocate, and lived down the hall from fellow sophmore Bill Gates. In 1980, he dropped out of the Stanford Graduate School of Business to join Microsoft.

Steve Ballmer was a Harvard roommate of Gates. He'd just joined Microsoft and would end up its third billionaire. Back then he was the only guy in the company with business training.

Steve Ballmer joined Microsoft on June 11, 1980, and became Microsoft's 30th employee, the first business manager hired by Gates.
Ballmer was initially offered a salary of $50,000 as well as a percentageof ownership of the company. When Microsoft was incorporated in 1981, Ballmer owned 8 percent of the company.
